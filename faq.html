Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below.

We offer standard and express shipping nationwide. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping takes 1-2 business days. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location.

We have a 14-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. If a product is defective, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery for a replacement or refund.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website where you can track your package in real-time.
